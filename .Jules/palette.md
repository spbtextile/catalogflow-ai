## 2024-05-20 - Adding Icons to Empty States
**Learning:** Pure text empty states are easy to overlook and can feel like a bug or missing content rather than an intentional design state. Adding a subtle icon (like an Inbox icon) paired with descriptive text significantly improves the visual hierarchy and clarifies that the area is intentionally empty.
**Action:** Always pair empty state text with a context-appropriate, low-contrast icon to make empty views feel intentional and designed.
